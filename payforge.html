Self-Service Finance

One Portal. Every Invoice. Any ERP.

Customers self-serve — view, download, and pay invoices, manage accounts, and control user access without calling your finance team.

Invoice Management

View, filter, and download PDF invoices in real time from Oracle EBS or Fusion AR. Paginated list with advanced filters, inline PDF viewer, status badges, and bulk ZIP export.

Oracle EBSFusion ARPDF ExportReal-time Sync

Online Payments

Pay single or multiple invoices in one checkout via JPMC, Stripe, Braintree, or Adyen. Tokenised payment methods — no card data stored on your servers. Instant PDF receipt with auto write-back to ERP cash application.

JPMCStripeBraintreeAdyen

Account & ZIP Management

Manage multiple business accounts and service ZIP codes with full audit trail. White-label branding per tenant with isolated data and per-client ERP connectors. Notification preferences and email alerts included.

Multi-tenantWhite-labelAudit Trail

User Access Control

Role-based access with Admin, Finance, and Viewer hierarchy. Invite by email, group-based access scoping, and full permission audit log. Integrates with Oracle IAM / IDCS, Azure AD, and Okta SSO — no IT involvement needed.

RBACOracle IAMAzure ADOkta SSO

Autopay & Payment Methods

Set up autopay mandates for recurring invoices. Save tokenised payment methods for future use — enable, disable, and modify at any time. Consolidated invoice downloads for multi-account clients.

AutopayTokenisationConsolidated

Security & Compliance

PCI-DSS v4.0 with card tokenisation, P2PE, and 3D Secure 2. GDPR & CCPA data residency controls. SOC 2 / ISO 27001 aligned. TLS 1.3, AES-256 at rest, WAF, and DDoS protection built in.

PCI-DSS v4GDPRSOC 2ISO 27001
