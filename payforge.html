Why PayForge Exists

The Problem We Solve

Oracle EBS and Fusion AR are powerful — but they leave a critical gap: your customers have no self-service way to view invoices or pay online. PayForge fills that gap.

Business Problem
What Oracle EBS & Fusion AR leave unresolved
No Customer-Facing Payment Portal
Oracle EBS and Fusion AR have no native portal where customers can log in, view their invoices, and pay online — forcing everything through your finance team.
Manual Invoice Chasing & AR Overhead
AR teams spend hours every week following up on overdue invoices by email and phone — with no audit trail, no automation, and no self-service option for customers.
Zero Customer Visibility
Customers cannot see what they owe, what's paid, or download invoice PDFs without contacting your team — creating friction and delaying settlement.
Manual GL Posting & Double Data Entry
Payments collected outside the ERP require manual posting and reconciliation — slow, error-prone, and a persistent drain on your finance team's capacity.
High DSO & Cash Flow Pressure
Without an instant online payment path, Days Sales Outstanding stays high — customers delay because paying is inconvenient, not because they cannot afford to.
PayForge — Our Solution
Production-grade · ERP-native · Gateway-agnostic
Branded Self-Service Portal on Oracle EBS & Fusion
A fully white-labelled, customer-facing portal — all data interactions between the portal UI and Oracle EBS/Fusion AR are orchestrated through OIC: invoice lists, account balances, PDF retrieval, and status updates all flow via OIC integration layers in real time.
Instant Payments via JPMC, Stripe, Braintree & Adyen
Customers pay single or multiple invoices in one checkout. The portal triggers a payment request via OIC to Payment Gateway, which processes the transaction securely. The gateway confirmation number is returned through OIC back to the portal UI and displayed on-screen instantly — full proof of payment, no waiting.
Automatic ERP Write-Back via Oracle OIC
Once Payment Gateway confirms the transaction, OIC automatically posts the confirmation number and payment record back to Oracle EBS/Fusion AR — triggering GL posting, cash application, and invoice status update in a single orchestrated flow. Zero manual reconciliation, zero double data entry.
Scheduled Autopay — Weekly, Monthly & Custom Cycles
Customers configure autopay on a weekly or monthly schedule — or a custom billing cycle. On each scheduled run, OIC orchestrates the full cycle: fetches open invoices from EBS, triggers payment to Payment Gateway, receives the confirmation number, writes the payment back to EBS/Fusion AR, and emails the receipt — completely automated, end to end.
Weekly Monthly Custom Cycle Auto Receipt & EBS Write-back
Enterprise IAM, RBAC & PCI-DSS v4.0 Compliance
Oracle IAM / IDCS, Azure AD, and Okta SSO out of the box. Role-based access (Admin, Finance, Viewer). PCI-DSS v4.0, GDPR, and SOC 2 aligned — enterprise-ready from day one.
