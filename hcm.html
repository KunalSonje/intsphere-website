Full HCM Implementation

End-to-end Human Capital Management — from core HR records to payroll, recruiting, talent management, and time & labor. We deliver the full hire-to-retire lifecycle on Oracle Fusion HCM Cloud, including complex guided onboarding, payroll compliance, and workforce automation.

Core HR
Employee records, org structures, positions, workforce modelling, and HR self-service across global entities.
Time & Labor
Time entry, shift scheduling, overtime rules, and payroll integration.
Fusion Payroll
Gross-to-net payroll processing with statutory compliance, WPS, and end-of-service benefits.
Fusion Recruiting Cloud Service
Requisition-to-offer management, candidate experience, job posting, interview scheduling, and offer automation.
Talent Management
Goal setting, performance reviews, succession planning, and workforce development.
Absence Management
Leave plans, accruals, approvals, and payroll integration.
Compensation
Salary structures, bonuses, and compensation planning aligned to budgets.
Workforce Health & Safety
Incident tracking, risk management, and compliance monitoring.
Workforce Scheduling
Demand-based scheduling and shift optimization tools.
Labor Optimization
