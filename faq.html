FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our Oracle Cloud consulting services and engagement models.

What Oracle Cloud services do you specialize in? +
We specialize in Oracle HCM Implementation, Oracle Integration Cloud (OIC), Visual Builder (VBCS), Process Automation (OPA), Fusion Applications technical extensions, Oracle CPQ, and Identity Cloud Service (IDCS). Our team also handles migrations from legacy SOA/OSB and PCS to modern PaaS.
Do you provide end-to-end implementation? +
Yes, from architecture design to development, testing, deployment, and post-go-live support. We work with your internal teams to ensure knowledge transfer and smooth adoption.
Can you help with Oracle Fusion SaaS extensions? +
Absolutely. We specialize in PaaS for SaaS — building custom extensions, integrations, and reports for Oracle Fusion HCM, ERP, and SCM using VBCS, OIC, and OTBI/BIP.
What is your experience with SOA/OSB to OIC migration? +
We have executed multiple complex migrations from legacy Oracle SOA Suite and OSB to modern OIC, ensuring minimal downtime and reusability of patterns.
Do you offer post-go-live managed services? +
Yes. Our managed services practice covers ongoing monitoring, incident resolution, performance tuning, and proactive maintenance. We offer flexible retainer models tailored to your team's capacity and coverage needs — from light-touch advisory to fully managed operations.
Can you work alongside our existing SI or internal team? +
Absolutely. We frequently operate as a specialist extension of client teams or alongside larger system integrators, providing deep Oracle PaaS or HCM expertise where it's needed most. We're collaborative by design and adapt to your governance and delivery model.
What industries do you serve? +
We work across a broad range of industries including financial services, retail, manufacturing, healthcare, public sector, real estate, and professional services. Our Oracle expertise is platform-deep rather than industry-narrow, and we apply best practices drawn from diverse enterprise environments to every engagement.
How can we engage your consulting team? +
Simply click "Contact Us" or "Talk to an Expert" to schedule a discovery call. We'll understand your needs and propose a tailored engagement model.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to discuss your specific Oracle Cloud requirements and how we can help.
